Benefits of Using App

Self Measuring

  • No need to ask your friend for help anymore, on those "hard to reach" places
  • No awkwardness or trying to give directions to others
  • Measure any part of your body with precision

Simple and Clear

  • Especially great for novices, students, and for those needing a refresher
  • Easy language and lexicon of terms provided
  • Reading and analyzing patterns made easy with fields auto-populated

No Guesswork

  • Minimize wasted material
  • Budgeting made more precise by knowing exactly what is needed
  • Calculate precise amounts of fabric needed

On the Go Portability

  • Shopping lists generated from projects
  • Organize everything by project
  • Mobile peripheral for quick reference

Staying Organized

  • Keep records of supplies owned and needed
  • Collect and maintain a library of patterns
  • Browse a catalog of patterns to buy instantly, keeping them within your library
